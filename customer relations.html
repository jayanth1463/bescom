24*7 SMS facility

Consumer can send SMS to 9243150000 to register their complaint in the format given below:

BESCOM < Sub- division code> < Nature of complaint> Ex: BESCOM N2 No Power Supply since 10.10 PM

The functioning of application is as follows:

Once the SMS is received, consumer gets back the acknowledgment message with ticket number. The application forwards complaint message to the concerned Sub-Divisional AEE, EE for necessary action.
